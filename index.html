Our Therapy Modes

Three powerful approaches to meet different rehabilitation needs

Enhanced Physical Therapy (EPT)

Traditional exercises enhanced with AI-powered real-time form correction and progress tracking.

Best for: Post-surgery recovery, injury rehabilitation

Gamified Physical Therapy (GPT)

Turn therapy into engaging games that motivate patients while ensuring proper movement patterns.

Best for: Pediatric therapy, long-term conditions

Interactive Therapeutic Systems (ITS)

Combines physical movement with cognitive challenges for comprehensive neurological rehabilitation.

Best for: Stroke recovery, neurological conditions

Speech Improvement Therapy (SIT)

Voice-driven games and pronunciation exercises designed to enhance speech clarity and confidence.

Best for: Articulation training, post-stroke speech recovery, speech delays
